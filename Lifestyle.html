What are lifestyle diseases ?

Lifestyle diseases are ailments that are primarily based on the day to day habits of people

Why should you bother ?

Lifestyle diseases result in development of chronic diseases,heart dieases and stroke

Common lifestyle diseases

  • Asthma
  • Chronic liver disease
  • Heart disease
  • Hypertension
  • Obesity
  • type 2 diabetes

Causes

  • Drug abuse and
  • Tobacco smoking
  • Alcohol drinking
  • Lack of exercise
  • Impact of behavioural factors unemployment, unsafe life etc

Prevention

  • Reduction in smoking of tobacco
  • Overweightness and obesity can be prevented through healthy eating and exercise
  • Moderate exercise includes a brisk walk, swim, bike ride
  • Reduce intake of alcohol and red meats
